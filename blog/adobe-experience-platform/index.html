Adobe Experience Platform Insights

Master AEP, Real-Time CDP, and unified customer profiles with expert guides from our Adobe Experience Platform consultants.

Adobe Experience Platform (AEP) is Adobe's foundation for customer experience management, providing unified customer profiles, real-time data processing, and AI-powered insights. Built to handle petabyte-scale data, AEP powers Real-Time CDP, Customer Journey Analytics, and Journey Optimizer.

Our guides cover the practical challenges of AEP implementation—from XDM schema design and identity resolution to preventing identity graph collapse in production. Whether you're integrating data sources, building audience segments, or activating profiles across destinations, we share enterprise-tested approaches that deliver results.
