Sales Cloud is Salesforce's CRM for sales teams—managing leads, opportunities, accounts, and pipeline. Marketing Cloud is for marketers—handling email campaigns, journey orchestration, social media, and advertising. WegWorks implements both and ensures they work together, syncing marketing engagement data with sales records for complete customer visibility.

Salesforce Data Cloud (formerly Salesforce CDP) unifies customer data from all sources into real-time profiles for segmentation and activation. It's ideal when you need to combine Salesforce data with external sources (web analytics, mobile apps, data warehouses) for unified customer views. WegWorks implements Data Cloud and connects it to Marketing Cloud, Service Cloud, and external platforms.
