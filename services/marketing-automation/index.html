WegWorks provides comprehensive marketing automation services including platform implementation, customer journey design, email program development, segmentation and targeting, integration with CRM and CDP systems, and reporting and optimization frameworks.

WegWorks designs journeys by mapping customer lifecycle stages, defining trigger events (purchases, sign-ups, behaviors), and creating branching logic based on engagement and attributes. We implement journeys in tools like Adobe Campaign, Marketo, HubSpot, or Braze—configuring wait steps, decision splits, channel selection, and personalization at each touchpoint.

Marketo (Adobe) is enterprise-grade B2B automation with advanced lead scoring, account-based marketing, and revenue attribution. HubSpot offers an all-in-one platform (CRM + marketing + sales + service) that's easier to use and great for SMB/mid-market. WegWorks evaluates your needs—team size, budget, integration requirements, and use cases—to recommend the right platform.

WegWorks configures bi-directional sync between your automation platform and CRM (Salesforce, HubSpot CRM, Dynamics). We map lead/contact fields, sync engagement data (email opens, clicks, page visits) to CRM records, configure lead scoring that updates in real-time, and ensure MQLs flow properly to sales with full context.

Basic implementations (email setup, CRM integration, simple journeys) take 4-6 weeks. Full implementations with complex journeys, lead scoring, multiple channels, and integrations take 8-12 weeks. Enterprise Marketo or Adobe Campaign deployments may take 3-4 months. WegWorks prioritizes launching core programs quickly while building advanced capabilities over time.

Lead scoring assigns points to leads based on demographics (title, company size, industry) and behaviors (email engagement, website visits, content downloads). WegWorks designs scoring models aligned with your sales process, implements them in your automation platform, and creates MQL thresholds that trigger sales handoff—ensuring sales focuses on the most qualified prospects.
