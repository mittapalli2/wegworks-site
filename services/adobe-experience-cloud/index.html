Adobe Experience Platform (AEP) is the foundation that powers Real-Time CDP, Journey Optimizer, and Customer Journey Analytics. It provides unified customer profiles, real-time data collection, and AI-powered insights. WegWorks implements AEP alongside traditional Experience Cloud products for organizations seeking next-generation customer data capabilities.

Adobe Target uses Analytics segments and behavioral data to power personalization and A/B testing. WegWorks configures Analytics for Target (A4T) integration, enabling you to use Analytics as the reporting source for Target activities. This provides unified measurement of personalization impact and allows you to build audiences in Analytics that activate in Target for real-time targeting.

Adobe Campaign is a traditional cross-channel campaign management tool for batch-based marketing with strong email and direct mail capabilities. Journey Optimizer is built on Adobe Experience Platform and enables real-time, event-triggered journey orchestration with AI-powered decisioning. WegWorks helps you choose the right solution—or implement both—based on your use cases.

Implementation timelines vary by scope. Single product implementations (Analytics, Target) typically take 4-8 weeks. Multi-product implementations with integrations take 3-6 months. Full AEM Sites implementations can take 6-12 months depending on complexity. WegWorks uses phased approaches to deliver value incrementally while building toward your complete vision.
